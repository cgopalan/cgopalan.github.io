Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Donate Your Gift ("the app") is designed to help users organize and share charity information.

Information We Collect

  • The app stores charity names, URLs, groups, and selection preferences entered by you.
  • This information is stored locally on your device using iOS local storage.
  • We do not require account creation.

How Information Is Used

  • Your data is used only to display and share your charity lists within the app.
  • When you choose to share via text, email, or Share Sheet, content is sent using services you select.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Any sharing happens only when you explicitly choose a sharing action in the app.

Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or uninstall the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: appdonateyourgift@gmail.com
