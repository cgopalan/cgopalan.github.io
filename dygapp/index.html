Donate Your Gift

Turn birthday gifts into donations.

Donate Your Gift helps you build and share a list of charities so friends and family can donate instead of buying gifts.

Key Features

  • Create and organize charities into multiple groups.
  • Add, edit, check, and delete charity entries.
  • Send only selected charities via text message or email.
  • Share quickly using the iOS Share Sheet.
